\item{\textbf{Question 2.42:}}\\

Construct ABCD, where $ AB=4, BC=5, CD=6.5, {\angle}B=105^{\circ}, {\angle}C=80^{\circ}$.\\

\item{\textbf{Solution:}\vspace{4}\\
\hspace{3} Given, AB=4, BC=5, CD=6.5, {\angle}B=105^{\circ}, {\angle}C=80^{\circ}.\\

Now,\\

\textbf{Steps of Construction.}

$

1: Draw a line AB=4 cm.\\

2: From point B draw a line BC= 5 cm such that {\angle}ABC=105^{\circ}.\\

3: From point C draw a line CD= 6.5 cm such that {\angle}BCD=80^{\circ}.\\

4: Join DA and we get required ABCD.\\
$

\node at (4,-0.3){$B$};
